Funding sources for undergraduate research in ecology and evolution

Last updated on Jun 24, 2020 2 min read Resources

Resources

For students

Broad

Undergraduate Teaching Assistanceships

  • Opportunities vary per university, but check with your department and work-study websites for information. Sometimes these opportunities aren’t advised well- if you have a class where you would like to do this, it doesn’t hurt to ask the instructor about opportunities!

  • Here is an example program: University of Texas College of Natural Sciences NSF Research Experiences for Undergraduates
    American Physiology Society Undergraduate Summer Research Fellowship

  • $4,000 stipend, up to $1,300 in EB travel expenses and a one year complimentary APS membership. 10 week position.

Society of Wetland Scientists Student Research Grant
Bat Conservation International Student Research Scholarship

  • Opens in October

Garden Club of America Scholarships

  • There are a variety of scholarships available
  • Subject list: Botany, Coastal Wetland Studies, Conservation & Ecological Restoration, Desert Studies, Garden History & Design, Horticulture & Related Fields, International Work & Study, Landscape Architecture, Native Bird Habitat, Pollinator Research, Summer Environmental Study, Urban Forestry

National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration Ernest F. Hollings Undergraduate Scholarship

  • Includes academic assistance (up to $9500 per year) for two years and a 10-week paid summer internship ($700 per week)

Sigma Xi Grants in Aid of Research

Human-Environment Regional Observatory Fellowship

  • Eight week stipend and academic credit

Local

Hudson River Foundation Polgar Fellowship

  • $5000 summertime grant in New York and New Jersey area

For faculty

NSF Facilitating Research at Primarily Undergraduate Universities

R01 supplements for URM students are available:

If you are an URM student and your mentor has an active R01, they could apply for a diversity supplement to cover your stipend, tuition, and up to 4K in institutional allowance. I also have a diversity supplement application to share. DM if interested.
